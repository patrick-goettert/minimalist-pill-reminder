Privacy

Minimalist Pill Reminder is designed to work without collecting your personal data.

  • No data collection: We do not collect, store, or sell personal information.
  • No tracking: We do not track you across apps or websites, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
  • On-device only: Your medications, schedules, and preferences are stored only on your device using Apple’s standard on-device storage.
  • Notifications: Reminder notifications are generated locally on your device. If Privacy Mode is enabled, medication names are hidden.
  • No accounts: The app does not require sign-in and does not create user accounts.
  • Support contact: If you contact us for support, we only use the information you provide to respond to your request.

This app complies with Apple’s App Store privacy guidelines.
