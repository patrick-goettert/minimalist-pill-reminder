Privacy Policy

Minimalist Pill Reminder is designed to respect your privacy. The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

Data Collection

Minimalist Pill Reminder does not collect any personal or usage data. We do not operate servers, analytics systems, or advertising services.

On-Device Storage

All medications, schedules, reminders, and user preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple’s standard on-device storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.
